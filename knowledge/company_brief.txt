Acme Robotics — Company Brief (sample knowledge file)

Mission
-------
Acme Robotics builds open-source perception stacks for warehouse automation.
We focus on edge-deployable computer vision and lightweight SLAM.

Products
--------
- AcmeVision: an Apache-2.0 perception SDK targeting Jetson Orin and Coral.
- AcmeFleet: a hosted fleet-management dashboard for autonomous mobile robots.
- AcmeSense: an indoor mmWave radar module for mixed lighting conditions.

Audience
--------
- Robotics engineers building pick-and-place or AMR fleets.
- Operations teams running 50+ robots per facility.
- Open-source contributors interested in low-power vision.

Recent context
--------------
- Q1 2026: shipped AcmeVision 3.0 with on-device VLM fallback (~80MB).
- Q1 2026: AcmeFleet entered closed beta with 4 logistics partners.
- Q4 2025: open-sourced our depth-completion model (MIT license).
